Egg Factory

Game Type: SRPG

Why Abandoned: UNFUN

Description

A silly resource management sim where you run a hyper-automated egg factory, balancing chicken happiness with ruthless productivity.

Abandonment Details

The gameplay loop felt too repetitive and lacked meaningful decisions.

Future Considerations

Could be revived with more sabotage mechanics and weird upgrades.
