Dreamgirl Horror

Game Type: AVG

Why Abandoned: BAD_FEEDBACK

Description

A psychological horror game set in a haunted sleep clinic where dreams bleed into reality. The player must navigate between dream and waking worlds to uncover a forgotten trauma.

Abandonment Details

Narrative complexity and dream logic systems became too difficult to design and test effectively.

Future Considerations

Could be rebooted as a linear visual novel with branching outcomes.
