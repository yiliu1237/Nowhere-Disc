Monster Milk Tea

Game Type: EDU / ACT

Why Abandoned: TOO_COMPLEX

Description

A fast-paced drink shop simulator where each customer is a different monster with unique quirks. You mix drinks using keyboard combos and monster psychology.

Abandonment Details

Core interaction system was too complicated and balancing monster traits was a nightmare.

Future Considerations

Could be remade as a card-based timing game or a mobile tap rhythm game.
